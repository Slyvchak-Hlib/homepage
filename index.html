Ryu is famous street fighter, he travels the world to improve his martial arts skills and sees the meaning of his life in this, his approach to both training and traveling is very responsible. Now he wants to create a web-site to promote his brand’s products. The product will most likely be T-shirts, nunchucks, and other such things.
